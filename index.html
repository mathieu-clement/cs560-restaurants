San Francisco Restaurants: Inspection Scores and Yelp Ratings

Mathieu Clement, Byron Han

This website allows the exploration of the inspection scores published for each restaurant in the City and County of San Francisco, California. When you are done exploring, take a look at some studies we have done trying to find correlations between inspection scores and the ratings on Yelp, and whether restaurants offering certain cuisines have higher scores than others.

Inspection data is published weekly by the San Francisco Health Department and is freely available online. Restaurant categories, geocoordinates and Yelp ratings are extracted from public Yelp APIs. The use of the Yelp Data provided on this website is subject to Yelp Terms and Conditions.

These districts are those established by the San Francisco Health Department. The name of the district can be seen by hovering over the map.
